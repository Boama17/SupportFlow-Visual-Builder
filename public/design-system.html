SupportFlow Design System

A warm, premium interface language for a visual support-flow builder that turns complex conversation design into a clear and confident experience.

1. Product Vision

Purpose

SupportFlow enables support teams to design, test, and refine conversational journeys without relying on spreadsheets or technical tools.

Business Value

It shortens the design cycle, improves consistency, and gives teams a clearer way to manage customer support experiences at scale.

2. Visual Language

Soft Cream

#ffe3cc

Used for light surfaces, soft highlights, and subtle contrast.

Warm Sand

#e7a46e

Used for secondary accents and friendly UI emphasis.

Amber

#dd8136

Used for active states, supporting actions, and interactive highlights.

Primary Orange

#fb7507

Used for key actions, primary node states, and core focal points.

Surface Panel

#23160d

Used for elevated cards, panels, and the main UI shell.

Deep Background

#17110b

Used for the full app background to create contrast and depth.

3. Interface Components

Buttons and Actions

Rounded buttons, soft depth, and clear contrast guide users toward the next step.

Node Variants

Start Question Response End

4. Wildcard Feature

Why it matters

  • It turns the builder into a thinking assistant rather than a static diagram tool.
  • It highlights weak branches, dead-end paths, and strong continuation points.
  • It helps non-technical users make better design decisions without needing extra training.

Business impact

  • Reduces rework by surfacing design issues earlier.
  • Improves support quality by keeping flows more consistent and intentional.
  • Supports faster scaling as the number of flows and conversations grows.

5. Design Principles

01

Clarity

Every interaction is easy to understand, with strong hierarchy and obvious visual structure.

02

Warmth

The orange palette feels energetic and modern while remaining approachable for business users.

03

Confidence

Rounded surfaces and polished elevation create a premium product experience that feels finished.
